Call to Action: Animal Cruelty in the United States

Why do States with more pet owners have more animal abuse incidents?

This is the question everyone should be asking. Although the pattern makes sense logically, that doesn't answer why the states have little to no animal protection laws. States with more pet owners should make it their priority to protect the animals and pets that call each state their home.

What can be done to change this?

Since this is a matter of the states independent governments, nothing can really be done by the citizens of the United States. The only thing we can do is vote for politicians who support animal protection laws and keep talking about how big of a problem this is.

Animals deserve love and respect

Animals hold a very pure place in everyones hearts and it's saddening to think of them getting hurt. As citizens of the United States it is our duty to step up and try give those animals rights.
